# Theorems

$$\bigtriangleup{ABC}$$ 의 결합 조건:

$$\overline{BC} < \overline{AB} + \overline{AC}$$ 성립

## Triangle inequalities theorem

(1)
$$ -|a| \leq a \leq |a| $$

(2)
$$|a+b| \leq |a| + |b|$$

(3)
$$|a-b| \leq |a| + |b|$$

(4)
$$||a|-|b|| \leq |a-b|$$

## Special inequalities

(1)
$$-|\theta| \leq sin\theta \leq |\theta|$$

(2)
$$-|\theta| \leq 1 - cos \theta \leq |\theta|$$
